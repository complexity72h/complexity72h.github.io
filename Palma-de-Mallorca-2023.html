APPLY

Send your CV and a motivation letter (max 1 page) to complexity72h [at] gmail [dot] com using as email subject: your_family_name with/without accommodation (with accommodation: if you would need accommodation at IFISC)

Motivation letter guidelines:
Tell us who you are, what you are currently doing, what you hope to take home from the workshop, and how you can contribute to it. Don't worry if your cv's not fat. Complexity72h is looking for committed, hardworking and curious people!

Selection process:
In order to keep a good participant-to-tutor ratio, and form groups of optimal size, a maximum of about 40 participants  will be selected. We aim at having participants coming from different stages of their career: Master students, PhD students, and postdocs. We thus encourage Master and young PhD students to apply!
